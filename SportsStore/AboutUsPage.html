MatterHackers is based around the connective passion and dedication for technology to allow anyone to create anything! Our company is based around the premise that anyone from educators and engineers to manufacturers and tinkerers can create using our 3D printers, filament, and more. Only the highest quality materials, machines, and accessories are provided, so you know right off the bat that you are getting everything you need at the best market value with the highest approval ratings. Our dedication does not stop at our products, our customer service team is here Monday through Friday to assist with any questions or building help you may need. Just send us an email, give us a call, or schedule an appointment to visit our showroom located in Southern California. We want to make sure you have all the tools and information you need to make your creativity soar!
